Mini Tiller and Digger Machine

The "Mini Tiller and Digger Machine" is a versatile agricultural project designed for small-scale farming and gardening. This compact machine serves as both a tiller for soil cultivation and a digger for excavation tasks. With its user-friendly design, lightweight construction, and adjustable settings, it optimizes efficiency in various agricultural environments. The project aims to enhance productivity while minimizing environmental impact, offering a practical solution for farmers and gardeners in tight spaces.

Research

Investigation of Mechanical properties of ratttan and date palm fiber and fiber reinforced hybrid composite.

The thesis, "Investigation of Mechanical Properties of Rattan and Date Palm Fiber and Fiber-Reinforced Hybrid Composite," delves into the study of materials crucial for composite fabrication. Focusing on rattan and date palm fibers, the research aims to explore their individual mechanical characteristics and subsequently assess the performance of a hybrid composite reinforced with these fibers. This investigation contributes valuable insights into the potential applications of natural fibers in composite materials, aiming to advance sustainable and durable solutions in various industries.
