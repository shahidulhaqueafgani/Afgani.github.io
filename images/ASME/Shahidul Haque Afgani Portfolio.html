Who I Am

Passionate about the field of mechanical engineering. With a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Mechanical Engineering from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology, I've embarked on a journey driven by my unwavering enthusiasm for innovation and problem-solving. My educational background has instilled in me a strong foundation in mechanical engineering, and it has ignited a curiosity that propels me forward. The knowledge and skills I've acquired during my academic journey have not only provided me with a comprehensive understanding of mechanical systems but have also fueled my desire to explore the limitless possibilities within this dynamic field.
My higher educational endeavors began at Notre Dame College, where I completed my Higher Secondary School Certificate, focusing on the Science stream. This phase of my academic life taught me the importance of discipline and a holistic educational background. Before these significant educational milestones, my journey commenced at Titas Gas Adarsha High School, introducing me to the world of learning and sparking my initial interest in the wonders of knowledge.
Beyond my academic pursuits, I am not just an ardent mechanical engineer but also a dedicated sportsperson. My two great passions are cricket and football. Cricket, in particular, holds a special place in my heart, and it's my most favorite sport. I am proud to have achieved the title of "Intra-School Cricket Champion" and "Intra-Department Cricket Champion," showcasing my dedication to the sport. Additionally, my passion for football led my team to secure the title of "Intra-School Football Runners-up," a testament to our teamwork and dedication on the field.
I firmly believe in the importance of a well-rounded life, and these sporting interests keep me physically active, foster teamwork, and provide a refreshing balance to my daily routines. Guided by values of integrity, determination, and empathy, I am dedicated to lifelong learning, self-improvement, and making a positive impact in the world.
My journey is an ever-evolving story, and I eagerly look forward to the opportunities, challenges, and experiences that lie ahead. I am excited to continue my personal and professional growth, connect with fellow enthusiasts, and contribute to the ever-advancing world of mechanical engineering while pursuing my passion as a sportsperson, with a special love for cricket.

Leadership Experience

Leadership experience is a transformative journey that molds character and forges vision. It's not merely about titles and positions,but rather about the profound impact one life can have on another. This journey has played a pivotal role in shaping who I am today. It's taught me the power of influence, empathy, and unwavering determination. Leadership experience has been instrumental in my personal growth, resilience, and unwavering commitment to making a meaningful impact.

Deputy Team Leader of Chassis Design & Fabrication Team, Circuit Maesters

Circuit Maesters has aimed to build the first driverless car of Bangladesh, and participate in the Formula Student UK 2022 Artificial Intelligence segment. The team has presented their plan/roadmap to the respected faculty members of the Dept of EEE, Aust and later they have also presented their goals to the respected Vice-Chancellor sir of AUST and got appreciated by everyone. The team has received numerous motivational, technical and financial support from Aust and has been continuously working towards building the perfect driverless car.

Mentor, Hult Prize Foundation

Being a mentor for the Hult Prize at AUST has been a truly enriching experience for me. It's an opportunity where I get to guide and inspire young, passionate minds in their journey to become social entrepreneurs. My role involves sharing knowledge, offering support, and helping these students shape innovative solutions to significant global challenges.
As a mentor, I've had the privilege of witnessing the incredible transformation of these budding entrepreneurs into future change-makers. It's a gratifying experience to contribute to their growth and see them working towards positive social impact. This role has not only allowed me to share my expertise but has also fostered my own leadership skills, creativity, and a deep commitment to creating a better, more sustainable future.

Chairperson, ASME AUST Student Chapter

Serving as the Chairperson of the ASME AUST Student Chapter has been an incredibly rewarding and fulfilling experience. In this role, I've had the opportunity to lead and work with a dedicated team of engineering enthusiasts, fostering a community of like-minded individuals passionate about mechanical engineering. As Chairperson, I've overseen various initiatives, workshops, and events that promote the exchange of knowledge, creativity, and innovation within our chapter. It's been a platform where I've not only honed my leadership and organizational skills but also learned the importance of teamwork and effective communication.
This experience has not only been instrumental in my personal growth but has also allowed me to contribute to the broader academic and professional development of fellow students in the field. It's a role that has empowered me to make a positive impact and inspire others to pursue their engineering aspirations.

Campus Director,Hult Prize at AUST

As the Campus Director of the Hult Prize at AUST for two consecutive years, I've had the privilege of leading an initiative that's achieved remarkable growth. Under my leadership, we saw a record-breaking registration of over 100 teams, marking a significant milestone in the history of the event at our campus. Additionally, we had the honor of hosting international judges, and it was a rewarding experience to have convinced and brought them on board. It's been an incredible journey, filled with challenges and achievements, where I've developed invaluable organizational and leadership skills. This role has not only pushed boundaries but has also inspired innovation and social impact among the students. It's a testament to our collective dedication to fostering positive change and creating a platform for aspiring social entrepreneurs.

President, ASHRAE AUST Student Branch

As the founder and founding president of the ASHRAE AUST Student Branch, I had the privilege of pioneering this esteemed organization at our university. This groundbreaking role enabled me to establish and lead a community of aspiring engineers with a deep interest in heating, refrigeration, and air conditioning. It has been an enriching journey, allowing me to develop essential leadership, teamwork, and organizational skills. This experience not only contributed to my personal growth but also opened doors for fellow students to explore the dynamic field of HVAC&R engineering.
However, it's important to note that due to the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, my ability to perform my duties effectively was somewhat hindered. Despite these unprecedented circumstances, our branch persevered and adapted to the new normal, showcasing our resilience and dedication to our mission.

Vice Chair, IMechE AUST Student Chapter

Taking the helm as the founding Vice Chair of the IMechE AUST Student Chapter, I had the distinct privilege of introducing this esteemed chapter to our university. In this pioneering role, I played a vital part in the establishment and nurturing of a community of aspiring mechanical engineers.
Throughout my tenure, we kickstarted a series of engaging events, workshops, and technical sessions, providing a platform for the exchange of engineering knowledge and innovative ideas within our chapter. This journey was marked by significant personal and professional growth, as I sharpened my leadership and organizational skills. It wasn't just my own development that saw enrichment; my fellow students were also presented with a valuable opportunity to delve into the world of mechanical engineering.

Team Ambassador, Hult Prize Foundation

In my role as the Team Ambassador for the Hult Prize Foundation at Kuala Lumpur Regionals, I had the opportunity to collaborate with international teammates for the first time. This experience was a profound journey into cross-cultural teamwork, offering insights into global cooperation, effective communication, and adaptability.
Working with teammates from diverse backgrounds, I gained a deeper appreciation for different perspectives and cultures. This experience not only expanded my professional horizons but also enriched me personally. It was a transformative experience that enhanced my global awareness and teamwork skills.

Assistant General Secretary, Notre Dame Science Club

Kicking off my leadership journey, my role as the Assistant General Secretary of the Notre Dame Science Club was where it all began.
